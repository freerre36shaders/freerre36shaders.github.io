KappaPT

What is KappaPT?

Some of you may already know the Kappa Shader of RRe36's, this is a very similar pack but instead of relying on the vanilla lightmap it is using world-space path-tracing to fully replace the lightmap. It delivers accurate blocklight emission, skylight occlusion, as well as global illumination through that.
