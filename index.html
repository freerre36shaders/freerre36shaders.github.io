RRe36's Shaders for Free

Welcome to the homepage!

Here, you can find all of RRe36's shaderpacks, even the paid ones, all for completely free.

RRe36's Shaderpacks

FastPBR Shader
A fast and high-quality shaderpack with realistic visuals and PBR atmospherics.

Kappa Shader
A high-quality realistic, yet also somewhat stylized shaderpack.

KappaPT
Kappa Shader's path-traced cousin, featuring bleeding-edge path-traced lighting.

Nostalgia Shader
Capturing the essence of old shaderpacks to create a stylized and modern Minecraft look.

NostalgiaVX
A more advanced version of Nostalgia Shader, featuring path-traced colored lighting using voxels.

Potato Shader
A very lightweight shaderpack that is still fancy in its own way.

Simplicissimus Shader
A shader that is about as simple as it gets.

Soft Voxels
A minimalistic and performant path-traced shaderpack.

Soft Voxels Lite
A cut-down version of Soft Voxels.

UShader
A loose recreation of an old SEUS mod of RRe36's, but without using any of its code.

Vanilla Plus Shader
A shaderpack that gives Minecraft a modern visual overhaul, while still staying faithful to its original look.
